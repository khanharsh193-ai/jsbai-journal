About the Journal Open Access

The Journal of Sustainable Biosciences and Agricultural Innovation (JSBAI) is a quarterly, double-blind peer-reviewed journal established in 2018 under BioAgri Academic Publishers Pvt. Ltd. The journal provides a rigorous platform for interdisciplinary research at the intersection of biological sciences and agricultural systems, with an emphasis on sustainability, food security, and technology-driven solutions.

JSBAI is indexed in Scopus (since 2020), NAAS-rated at 6.52 (2024), and listed on the UGC-CARE List I. The journal observes a strict zero-APC policy for authors with corresponding affiliations at Indian universities and research institutes.

Author Guidelines

Manuscript Preparation

  • Prepare manuscripts in Microsoft Word (.docx) using Times New Roman 12pt, double-spaced, A4 page size with 2.5 cm margins.
  • Sections: Title Page → Abstract → Keywords → Introduction → Materials & Methods → Results → Discussion → Conclusion → Acknowledgements → References.
  • Review Articles: Title Page → Abstract → Keywords → Introduction → Thematic Sections → Conclusions → Future Directions → References.
  • Word limit: Original Research 5,000–8,000; Review Articles 6,000–12,000; Short Communications 1,500–3,000 words.

Citation & Reference Style

  • JSBAI follows APA 7th Edition reference style throughout.
  • In-text citations: (Author, Year); (Author & Author, Year); (Author et al., Year) for three or more authors.
  • All references in a single alphabetically ordered list at the end of the manuscript. DOIs must be included where available.
  • Self-citation should not exceed 15% of total references.

Figures, Tables & Data

  • Figures: minimum 300 DPI (600 DPI recommended for micrographs); TIFF or high-resolution JPEG preferred.
  • Tables should be editable (Word format), not embedded as images.
  • Raw datasets should be deposited in a public repository (Zenodo, Figshare) and linked in the manuscript.

Peer Review Process

  • JSBAI employs double-blind peer review. Manuscripts are assigned to two independent referees with domain expertise.
  • Initial editorial screening: 5–7 working days. Full review turnaround: 6–10 weeks.
  • Revised manuscripts should be resubmitted within 60 days with a point-by-point response to reviewer comments.

Ethics & Plagiarism Policy

  • All submissions are screened using iThenticate. Manuscripts with similarity index above 15% (excluding references) will be returned without review.
  • Research involving human subjects or animals must include a statement of ethical approval from a recognised institutional review board.
  • JSBAI is a signatory to the COPE (Committee on Publication Ethics) guidelines.

Article Processing Charges

  • Indian Corresponding Authors: ₹0 — Fully waived for all institutional affiliations.
  • International Authors: USD 120 (standard); USD 60 for LMIC countries. Waivers available on request.
