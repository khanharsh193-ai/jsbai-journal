About the Journal Open Access

The Journal of Sustainable Biosciences and Agricultural Innovation (JSBAI) is a quarterly, double-blind peer-reviewed journal established in 2018 under BioAgri Academic Publishers Pvt. Ltd.

JSBAI is indexed in Scopus (since 2020), NAAS-rated at 6.52 (2024), and listed on the UGC-CARE List I. Zero APC for Indian institutional authors.

Author Guidelines

Manuscript Preparation

  • Prepare in Microsoft Word (.docx), Times New Roman 12pt, double-spaced, A4 with 2.5 cm margins.
  • Word limit: Original Research 5,000–8,000; Reviews 6,000–12,000; Short Communications 1,500–3,000 words.

Citation Style

  • JSBAI follows APA 7th Edition throughout. DOIs must be included where available.
  • Self-citation should not exceed 15% of total references.

Peer Review

  • Double-blind peer review. Initial screening: 5–7 working days. Full review: 6–10 weeks.

Article Processing Charges

  • Indian Corresponding Authors: ₹0 — Fully waived.
  • International Authors: USD 120 (standard); USD 60 for LMIC countries.
